Artist Statement

A concise yet lyrical statement about your process, materials, themes, and intentions.

My work is an archive of quiet transitions. I layer found papers, natural pigments, graphite, and photographs, sanding and sealing until traces of time emerge. The resulting surfaces hold small geographies—edges where memory softens. I search for a balance between absence and presence, inviting viewers to rest in the in‑between.
